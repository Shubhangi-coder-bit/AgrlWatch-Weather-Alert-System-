📋 GUIDELINES
Seasonal Safety Tips
01

Pre-Storm Field Prep

Clear irrigation channels, check drainage, secure structures, tie down equipment and polythene covers before heavy rain or storms arrive.

02

Heatwave Livestock Care

Shade all animals, ensure constant fresh water, reduce feed during peak heat (11AM–4PM). Watch for laboured breathing and lethargy as heat stress signs.

03

Post-Rain Disease Watch

Inspect crops for fungal infections, root rot, and waterlogging within 24–48 hours of heavy rain. Apply fungicide if symptoms appear early.

04

Smart Irrigation

Avoid irrigating 24 hours before predicted rain. Early morning irrigation reduces evaporation loss by up to 40% vs afternoon watering.

05

Frost Protection

Oct–Feb: when night temps forecast below 4°C, use smoke, mulching, or anti-frost sprays to protect sensitive winter crops like vegetables and fruit.

06

Spray Windows

Spray only when wind is below 15 km/h and no rain expected for 6+ hours. Early morning is ideal — better adhesion and zero drift.
